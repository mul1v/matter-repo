Welcome to Mater Hospital

About Us

The Hospital in accordance with the philosophy of the Sisters of Mercy aims to reach out to all members of the community irrespective of race, colour or creed, to continue the healing ministry of the church and preserve the heritage of the Sisters of Mercy.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional healthcare services with compassion, dignity, and respect. We are committed to promoting the health and well-being of our patients through advanced medical care, innovative treatments, and a supportive, healing environment. We strive to be a trusted healthcare leader, improving the lives of individuals and our community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading healthcare provider, known for excellence in patient care, innovation, and education. We strive to create a healthier community by delivering accessible, high-quality, and compassionate care while advancing medical research and training the next generation of healthcare leaders.
